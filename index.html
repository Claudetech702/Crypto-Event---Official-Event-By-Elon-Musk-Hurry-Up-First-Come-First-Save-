Frequently Asked Questions

FAQ

Find answers to common questions about our crypto event

How do I participate in the crypto event?

To participate, simply send your cryptocurrency (BTC, ETH, or DOGE) to the provided address. Minimum amounts are: 0.05 BTC, 1 ETH, or 10,000 DOGE. Your participation will be automatically registered on the blockchain.

Can I send funds from an exchange?

Yes, you can participate directly from exchanges like Binance, Coinbase, Kraken, or any other platform. However, we recommend sending from a personal wallet for faster processing and better control over your funds.

How long does it take to receive my doubled crypto?

Transactions are typically processed within 5-15 minutes after confirmation on the blockchain. During periods of high network congestion, it may take slightly longer. You can track your transaction using the provided TXID.

What networks are supported?

We support the native networks for each cryptocurrency:

  • Bitcoin (BTC) - Bitcoin Network
  • Ethereum (ETH) - Ethereum Network (ERC-20)
  • Dogecoin (DOGE) - Dogecoin Network
Sending through other networks may result in permanent loss of funds.

Is there a maximum participation limit?

Yes, the maximum participation limits are:

  • 15 BTC per transaction
  • 150 ETH per transaction
  • 1,000,000 DOGE per transaction
For larger amounts, please contact our support team.

How can I verify this is legitimate?

All transactions are recorded on the blockchain and can be verified publicly. We recommend:

  1. Checking our smart contract address (for ETH)
  2. Reviewing our transaction history
  3. Starting with a small test transaction
  4. Reading community feedback about our service
Never send more than you can afford to lose.

Important: Never share your private keys or seed phrase with anyone. Our team will never ask for this information. Always verify the wallet address before sending funds.
